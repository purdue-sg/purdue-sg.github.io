Media Posting Guidelines

  • Use the Post Request Form for all outward-facing posts (Instagram, Briefing, other socials).
  • Lead time: submit at least a week before desired publish date.
  • Complete info only: include event name, date/time, location, event information, contact, and approved graphics.
  • Branding: follow PSG and Purdue brand standards (colors, logos, fonts). Use the Templates & Assets card.
  • Accuracy: verify spelling, links, tag handles, and official names before submission.
  • Priority: student-impact events and time-sensitive items may be prioritized.
  • Approval: posts are scheduled by the Press team after review. Edits may be requested.
  • No personal promotions or unofficial fundraising without prior approval.

What to Include in Your Post Request

  • Short headline (under 60 characters) and a 1–2 sentence caption.
  • Date/time, location, and call-to-action (RSVP, register, buy tickets, etc.).
  • Square (1080×1080) and story (1080×1920) graphics, PNG/JPG preferred.
  • Alt text or a short description for accessibility.
  • Owner/contact for follow-ups and replies.
