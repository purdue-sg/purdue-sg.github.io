PSG Finance Rules

  • All purchases must be pre-approved by the PSG Treasurer before reimbursement is guaranteed.
  • Reimbursements must be submitted within 2 weeks of the purchase date.
  • You must provide itemized receipts, credit card summaries or bank statements are not sufficient.
  • Only expenses directly related to PSG events, travel, or operations will be reimbursed.
  • Food purchases must be approved in advance and follow university branding limitations (Coca-Cola Brands Only).
  • Travel expenses must comply with PSG travel policy and require documentation.
  • BOSO may require additional forms or verification, including vendor registration or tax forms.
  • When in doubt, reach out to the PSG Treasurer (Paul Springer) before making a purchase.
