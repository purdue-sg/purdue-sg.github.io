Absence Request Rules

  • All absences must be submitted at least 24 hours before the meeting.
  • Include your name, date of absence, and reason.
  • Emergency absences (illness, family emergency) may be submitted up to 1 hour before.
  • Unexcused absences will result in point deductions.
  • If you miss more than 3 meetings unexcused, you will be flagged by membership.
