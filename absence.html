BOD Absence Request Rules

As a member of the Board of Directors:

  • Submit the Qualtrics absence request form (link above) before the meeting you’ll miss.
  • Notify upper leadership: Executive Director (ED), Deputy Chief of Staff (DCOS), or Chief of Staff (COS).
  • Follow any additional instructions listed in the official Absence Policy (policy link above).

Senate Absence & Proxy Rules

As a Senator:

  • Submit the Qualtrics absence request form (link above).
  • Complete the Senate Proxy Report (card above) if you will designate a proxy.
  • Review the Proxy Report document for eligibility, deadlines, and required information.
