What Points Mean

Your accumulated points reflect your engagement and leadership in PSG. They factor into eligibility for executive positions, awards, and end‑of‑year recognition. The more points you earn, the more you demonstrate commitment to our mission and community impact. Anyone within PSG can earn points, Senators, BOD Members, Court Members.
