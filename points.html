Ways to Earn Points

  • Attend weekly PSG meetings
  • Volunteer at PSU‑sponsored events

  • Participate in special committees or task forces
  • Lead a project or initiative (e.g., community outreach)
  • Complete training workshops (leadership, diversity, etc.)
