Event Rules

Activity Planning Forms (APFs)

  • All APFs must be submitted 2.5 weeks in advance.
  • Submit an APF if you are spending money or doing anything more than a tabling event.
  • APFs are reviewed by Student Life Admin — keep it professional.
  • Be descriptive and submit only when all information is ready (no drip-feeding updates).
