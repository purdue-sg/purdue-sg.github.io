APFs

  • All APFs must be submitted 2.5 weeks in advance
  • You must submit an APF if you are spending money or doing anything more than a Tabling Event
  • APFs are reviewed by all Student Life Admin, so professionalism is very important
  • Be as descriptive as possible, only submit the APF once you have all information available; do not drip feed information
